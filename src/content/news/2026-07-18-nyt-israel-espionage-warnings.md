Israeli security agencies have implemented a broad array of measures to combat what they describe as a surge in online espionage. The focus is on preventing Iranian intelligence from recruiting local citizens via social media.

Public warnings and digital awareness campaigns have been rolled out across the country, highlighting the tactics used by foreign agents to approach unsuspecting individuals for information gathering.
