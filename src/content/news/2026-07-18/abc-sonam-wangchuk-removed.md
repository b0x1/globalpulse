Renowned Indian educational reformer and activist Sonam Wangchuk, who has been on a hunger strike, was forcibly relocated to a Delhi hospital by police officers following a court order regarding his health.

Wangchuk has been protesting against a massive nationwide exam leak scandal that has affected millions of students across India, demanding immediate accountability and reforms from the education ministry.
