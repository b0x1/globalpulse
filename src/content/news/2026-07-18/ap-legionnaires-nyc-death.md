New York City health authorities have confirmed the first death associated with an outbreak of Legionnaires' disease on the Upper East Side. The cluster of cases has grown to a total of 67 patients.

Environmental specialists are investigating cooling towers and water systems in the affected area to locate the source of the bacteria. Residents, particularly the elderly and immunocompromised, have been advised to seek immediate care if they exhibit symptoms.
