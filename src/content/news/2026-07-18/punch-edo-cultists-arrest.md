The Edo State Security Network, in collaboration with the Nigerian Police Force, has arrested 12 suspected members of a notorious cult group during a series of coordinated raids in Benin City.

Weaponry, communication devices, and various illicit substances were recovered during the operation. Authorities have vowed to continue the crackdown to restore peace and security to the affected residential areas.
