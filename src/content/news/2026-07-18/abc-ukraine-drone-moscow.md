A series of Ukrainian drone strikes targeted two expansive logistics warehouses belonging to a major Russian online retailer in Elektrostal, situated roughly 50 kilometers east of Moscow, local authorities confirmed.

The strikes triggered a massive blaze, with smoke columns visible for miles. Russian emergency services reported eight fatalities and dozens of injuries, marking one of the most significant drone attacks on industrial sites in the region.
