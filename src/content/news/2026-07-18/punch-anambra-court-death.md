An Anambra State High Court has sentenced two men to death by hanging after finding them guilty of the rape and subsequent murder of a mentally impaired teenage girl last year.

The presiding judge emphasized that the severe sentence should serve as a strong deterrent against gender-based violence and crimes targeting vulnerable members of society. Human rights advocates welcomed the verdict.
