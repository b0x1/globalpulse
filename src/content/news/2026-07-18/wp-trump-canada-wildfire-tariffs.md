President Donald Trump has warned Canada that he could impose heavy tariffs if the country does not take more aggressive action to control its seasonal forest fires, which have caused air quality warnings across multiple US states.

The threat has raised concerns among trade diplomats and economists, who warn that trade barriers could disrupt cross-border supply chains and further strain relations between Ottawa and Washington.
