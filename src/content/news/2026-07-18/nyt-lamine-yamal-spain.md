As the Spanish national soccer team prepares for the highly anticipated World Cup final, 19-year-old superstar Lamine Yamal has become the central figure of a broader social conversation.

Born to African immigrant parents, Yamal's meteoric rise on the pitch has triggered an intense national debate regarding multiculturalism and what it means to be Spanish in an increasingly diverse modern society.
