The Mediterranean diet has long been celebrated as a gold standard for heart and brain health. Recent studies continue to show that its focus on whole grains, healthy fats, and fresh produce can significantly improve longevity.

Dieticians suggest five simple adjustments to start, including swapping butter for olive oil, incorporating more legumes, and choosing fresh fruit over sugary desserts.
