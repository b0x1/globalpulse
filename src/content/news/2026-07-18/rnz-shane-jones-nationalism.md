Senior cabinet minister Shane Jones has explicitly positioned New Zealand First as a staunchly nationalist political force during his address at the party's annual conference, reiterating calls to restrict immigration levels.

Jones criticized current migration trends, claiming they put unsustainable pressure on domestic infrastructure, housing, and social services, sparking intense debate among coalition partners and opposition MPs.
