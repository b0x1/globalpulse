Tensions in the Middle East have escalated to a critical point as Iran threatened a "full-scale offensive" against US forces and allies in the region if Washington continues its campaign of targeted airstrikes.

The warning comes after several key strategic installations and transport corridors in the Gulf were targeted. International observers have urged both sides to show maximum restraint to prevent a wider conflict.
