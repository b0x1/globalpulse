The Reserve Bank of New Zealand's decision to maintain high interest rates has led to a protracted cooling in the residential real estate market.

According to the latest monthly report, median prices in Auckland and Wellington have fallen to their lowest points in two years, leaving sellers facing a challenging buyer-dominated market.
