The India Meteorological Department (IMD) declared on Sunday that the southwest monsoon has advanced to cover the entirety of the subcontinent ahead of schedule.

The widespread and consistent rainfall has triggered a surge in agricultural sowing, boosting expectations for a bountiful autumn harvest.
