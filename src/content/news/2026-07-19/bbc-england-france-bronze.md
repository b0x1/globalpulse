In one of the most high-scoring and chaotic games in World Cup history, England claimed the bronze medal with a stunning 6-4 victory over France.

Saka scored a hat-trick to lead the Three Lions, while Mbappe's goals for France were not enough to overcome the resilient English attack. The match ended a thrilling campaign for both sides.
