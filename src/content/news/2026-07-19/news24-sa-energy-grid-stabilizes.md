Eskom announced that South Africa's national power grid has shown unprecedented stability over the past week, thanks to the completion of crucial maintenance projects.

Additionally, several utility-scale solar and wind projects funded by the private sector have started feeding power into the grid, lowering the necessity for rotational power cuts.
