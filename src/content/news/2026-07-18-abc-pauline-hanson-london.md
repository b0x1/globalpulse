One Nation leader Pauline Hanson completed her European and British tour on a quiet note, delivering her keynote speech to a largely empty auditorium at the Conservative Political Action Conference (CPAC) in London.

Organizers estimated that only 120 attendees were present for the session, in contrast to the heavily crowded, high-energy events typical of the American version of the conservative gathering.
