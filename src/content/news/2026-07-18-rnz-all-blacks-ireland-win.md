The All Blacks delivered an outstanding performance against Ireland, securing a comprehensive victory at Auckland's Eden Park to extend their remarkable decades-long unbeaten run at the venue.

Despite a competitive first half, the New Zealand side ran away with the game in the final 30 minutes, scoring several unanswered tries to the delight of the sold-out home crowd.
