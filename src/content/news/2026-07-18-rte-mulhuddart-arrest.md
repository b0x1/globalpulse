Gardaí have arrested a second man in connection with the fatal assault of a young man in Mulhuddart, West Dublin, earlier this week. The suspect, aged in his late 20s, is being held at a local police station.

Forensic teams are continuing their examination of the crime scene. Investigators have appealed to anyone with dashcam footage or information about the incident to contact the investigation team.
